Rachel Pauley wears two hats:

1. **Founder/operator of District AI Index** (districtaiindex.com) — the K-12 AI tools directory we work on in this repo.
2. **Director of Technology at Raleigh County Schools, West Virginia** — her day job at a real WV public school district. Email: rpauley@k12.wv.us.

This dual role is load-bearing for outreach strategy: she has genuine peer credibility with other K-12 tech directors, especially in WV. Cold emails should use peer-to-peer framing ("Rachel here, DoT at Raleigh County…"), not vendor-pitch framing. Open rates and replies will be much higher than for any commercial sender.

For product decisions: when she says "what districts need," she's also telling you what *she* needs in her day job. Take it as both market signal and direct user need.

WV-specific context: The DOJ Title II ADA web/app rule has an April 26, 2027 compliance deadline for entities under 50,000 population — applies to most WV districts. VPAT/ACR documentation is the load-bearing artifact. The site has a `/vpat` tracker built specifically for this audience.
